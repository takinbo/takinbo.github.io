About

Tim Akinbo is a software designer based in Lagos, Nigeria. He grew up in the beautiful town of Jos, Plateau where he went to secondary school and later went to study Electrical Engineering at the Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife.

In 2008, he moved to Lagos and worked for a few years as a freelancer. As a freelancer, he worked on projects like EduERP - an Enterprise Resource Planning system for Educational Institutions and later got involved in the RapidSMS project. In 2010, he founded TimbaObjects - a software development firm.
